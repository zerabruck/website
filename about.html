About Our Company

Welcoming you to ECASN

ECASN is a non-profit community-based organization headquartered in Overland Park, Kansas, with a passionate team spread across various states including Oregon, Tennessee, Maryland, and Texas. Our core team members bring a wealth of experience and expertise, united by a common goal to make a difference.

Our Mission is clear: to Empower Ethiopian nonprofit organizations across North America for sustainable development in the communities they serve.

Our vision

Create a network of Ethiopian nonprofits

Our vision is to create a network of empowered Ethiopian nonprofits in North America that actively contribute to the sustainable development of Ethiopians, leaving a lasting legacy of progress and improved quality of life for all. Through collaboration, innovation, and dedication, we strive to build a brighter future for our community.

Ask What You Want To Know

Frequently Asked Questions

ECASN provides support to a wide range of Ethiopian nonprofit organizations across North America. This includes community development organizations, cultural associations, educational initiatives, healthcare programs, advocacy groups, and more.

ECASN offers a variety of services aimed at supporting the growth and effectiveness of Ethiopian nonprofit organizations. This includes capacity-building workshops, technical assistance, networking opportunities, advocacy efforts, and access to resources and tools. Whether you need help with fundraising strategies, program development, or community outreach, ECASN is here to help your organization succeed.

There are many ways to get involved with ECASN and support our mission. You can volunteer your time and expertise to help with events, workshops, or outreach efforts. You can also become a supporter by attending our events, spreading the word about ECASN, or making a donation to support our programs and initiatives.

ECASN stands out for its focus on the Ethiopian community and its commitment to grassroots empowerment. We understand the unique challenges and opportunities facing Ethiopian nonprofit organizations, and our services are tailored to address these specific needs. Additionally, ECASN fosters a culture of collaboration, innovation, and inclusivity, bringing together diverse voices and perspectives to drive positive change within the community.
