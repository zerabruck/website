OUR APPROACH

Capacity Building and Education

At ECASN, we believe that the progress of our community depends on how effectively we implement our initiatives. We are committed to empowering Ethiopian nonprofit organizations across North America by providing comprehensive support and resources.
Learn More Learn More Watch Video

Empowering Communities, Transforming FuturesTogether. Have a look at our events

Event One

Annual Conference 2024

ECASN hosted its inaugural Annual Conference in 2023, bringing together leaders and members of Ethiopian nonprofit organizations from across North America. The conference provided a platform for networking, collaboration, and knowledge-sharing, with workshops and panel discussions focused on capacity building, fundraising strategies, and community development.
Event Details Event Details
Event Two

Virtual Workshop Series - Grant Writing Essentials

In collaboration with experienced grant writers and fundraising experts, ECASN organized a virtual workshop series focused on Grant Writing Essentials. This series aimed to equip Ethiopian nonprofit leaders with the skills and knowledge needed to secure funding for their initiatives.
Event Details Event Details
Event Three

Community Outreach Day

ECASN coordinated a Community Outreach Day in partnership with local Ethiopian nonprofit organizations. Volunteers came together to engage in various outreach activities, including food drives, health screenings, and educational workshops. This event served to strengthen ties within the Ethiopian community, raise awareness about important issues, and provide direct support to those in need.
